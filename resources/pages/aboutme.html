GALA

Hi, I'm Gala!

Gala Nikolic, actress, director and model, currently based in Los Angeles, California.
Born in Rijeka, Croatia in 1997. where she finished a three-year Primary School for classical ballet and contemporary dance, and an English language acting group - Try Theatre.
Gala got her BA Acting degree from the Academy of Applied Arts at the University of Rijeka in the class of Rade Šerbedžija and Lenka Udovički.
While living in London, she attended masterclasses at LAMDA, National Youth Theatre and The Royal Conservatoire of Scotland.
Currently, she is studying acting at the Master of Fine Arts program at the California Institute of the Arts in LA.
She played significant roles in more than nine theater productions by Theatre Ulysess, HNK Ivan plemeniti Zajc and INK Pula and starred in the movie "Fishing and Fishermen's Conversations" directed by Milan Trenc.
Her debute in directing and screenplay is a critically acclaimed mono-play "Land of the Wolves".
